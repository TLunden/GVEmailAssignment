Home Buying 101

Free Seminar & Lunch

 

You may be closer to achieving home ownership than you think! Learn about the process of buying a new home during our free seminar and lunch at Belterra Model Center in Fresno.

 

At this event, experts in a number of fields will be providing the tips and advice you need to make your dreams of owning a new home a reality. Topics you will learn include:

  • Ways to improve your credit score
  • How to buy a home after a foreclosure or short sale
  • Which financing and assistance programs you may be eligible for
  • How today’s real estate market benefits you
  • How much house you can afford
  • And much more!

In addition, you will be able to talk to our Home Technology Experts about all of the latest advances that make today’s homes smarter than ever before.
